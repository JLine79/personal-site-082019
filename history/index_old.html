Education, Training & Experience

I have a degree in International Business with Italian, and more learn-by-doing-life-lessons than I can recount. I have experience and training in:

  • Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning
  • Leadership & collaboration
  • Data Visualisation & using data to make better decisions
  • Customer Friendly Writing & effective communication
  • Advocacy, policy development & evidence-based arguments
  • Front End Development & Coding (new skills on the way!)

Data

Lots of people seem to hate data. Not me. I love a good spreadsheet. Excel, Tableau, SPSS and Access are my friends.

I can dive into that mess of spreadsheets you've been squirrelling away, work out what your Google Analytics are telling you, or find some good old secondary datasets to provide you with some perspective, so that you can make confident, informed decisions.

Project Management

Project Management is the theme that has run most consistently through my career. I've run localisation projects for international clients, produced research, delivered funded projects and annual awards.

And, as a mother of 3 my life is basically never-ending project management (I know you know what I mean, fellow parents).

Basically, I have exceptional organisational skills. Inbox zero anyone?

Facilitation

I have worked with people enough to know you can't get anywhere without them. I took a course in facilitation, and practiced putting theory into practice.

I can help bring people together, bring views out into the open and navigate ways through differences to the common ground underneath. I will work with you to design and run sessions to draw out experiences, understand perspectives and reach decisions.

People

I'm somewhere between an introvert and an extrovert when it comes to people. In practice, this means that I can nurture conversations effectively, but then shut up long enough to hear and reflect on what's being said.

My professional history with people includes advocacy for affordable housing and community led housing, line management, event facilitation, network management and board membership.

Housing Expertise

Until very recently my career was focused on housing. I've been involved in many different things including:

  • Carrying out research
  • Strategic enabling
  • Running consultations & events
  • Delivering international awards
  • Working with & presenting to panels and expert groups

I'm also currently vice-chair of Leicestershire Rural Housing Association.
